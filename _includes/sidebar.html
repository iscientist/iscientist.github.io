About Us

The iScientist is McMaster University's undergraduate interdisciplinary research journal. We publish content representing a wide range of scientific fields, all of which is openly accessible. The iScientist publishes articles on a rolling basis online, which are compiled into annual volumes. The journal is run by a student-led team of volunteers.
